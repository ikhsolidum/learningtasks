Chili Con Carne

  • Chili Con Carne

Ingredients:
1. 1 red onion
2. 1 clove of garlic, finely chopped
3. 1 red pepper, roughly chopped
4. 1 green pepper, roughly chopped
5. 400g minced beef (i usually use lean)
6. 1 tbsp oil
7. 1 tin chopped tomatoes
8. 1 tin kidney beans, drained
9. 1 tbsp chill powder
10. 1 tbsp smoked paprika (or regular)
11. 1 tsp ground cumin
12. Salt and pepper to taste
13. Serve with rice, plain yoghurt and avocado
